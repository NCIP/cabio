caBIO Portlet Online Help
The caBIO Portlet allows scientific researchers to easily search, browse, and create reports for caBIO (Cancer Bioinformatics Infrastructure Objects) data within the caGrid Portal.
caBIO is a repository of data, compiled from multiple primary sources, that is useful in biomedical research. Using the Portlet within the caGrid Portal is an easy way to get started with caBIO.
