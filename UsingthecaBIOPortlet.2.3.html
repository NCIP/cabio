Searching caBIO Data
Both simple and templated search options are available in the caBIO Portlet. A simple search involves entering a term in the caBIO domain and clicking Search. A templated search provides users with pre-configured searches of caBIO data categorized by functionality.
See Understanding the caBIO Portlet for more information on caBIO data.
