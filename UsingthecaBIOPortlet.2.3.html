Searching caBIO Data
You can perform a simple or templated search in the caBIO Portlet. A simple search involves entering a term in the caBIO domain such as Gene, Chromosome, Sequence, SNP, Library, Clone, or Pathway and clicking Search. You can also retrieve more targeted search results by providing specific search criteria.
Templated searches are pre-configured searches of caBIO data that are categorized by functionality. Categories of templated searches include genome range queries, microarray annotations, genomic annotations, pathways, and cancer gene index.
See Understanding the caBIO Portlet for more information on caBIO data.
