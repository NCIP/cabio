Favorites tab
Create a list of help topics you want to return to frequently by navigating to the topic, clicking the Favorites tab at the top of the table of contents pane, and then clicking Add.
Divider line
To expand or narrow the width of the table of contents or topic pane, click and drag the divider between them.

Some words in this online help have pop-up definitions (yellow windows that pop up over the main text on the page) and are formatted as follows: dbSNP identifier. To see the definition in a pop-up window, hover your mouse over the word. To close the pop-up window, click anywhere in the online help. Additionally, the online help glossary is comprehensive, including definitions for these terms. Clicking on a word formatted as a pop-up definition (rather than only hovering your mouse over it) opens the glossary rather than a pop-up window.
