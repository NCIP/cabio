Creating A New Query
BIOgopher enables you to build complex queries against caBIO datasources. The result of running these queries will be a spreadsheet. To start building your query you must first decide, which caBIO object will be the subject of each row in your final spreadsheet? This will be the object that you select from the drop-down list in step A, on the left.

Since you could create more than one query during your session, you must give each new query a name, preferably something meaningful. Type this name into the textbox in step B, on the left. Then, press Create, to begin designing your query.

Working With An Existing Query
When you point your browser to BIOgopher, you begin a session. A BIOgopher session lasts until either you close your browser or you don't do anything with BIOgopher for three hours.

Within your session, you can create as many queries as you like. Once your session ends, your queries will be erased.

To work with an existing query, select it from the drop-down list in step A of the Work With An Existing Query section, to the left. Then, press one of the buttons in step B.
